Privacy

Last updated August 3, 2023


Introduction

LaughNow respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data. The present policy describes:

  • The types of information we may collect or that you may provide when you install, register with, access, or use the LaughNow app or our website.

  • Our practices for collecting and protecting that information.

This policy applies only to information we collect in our app and in our website.

Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data security. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not install or use the LaughNow app.

By installing or using our app, you agree to this privacy policy. This policy may update from time to time. Your continued use of LaughNow after we modify this policy means you accept those changes. Please check the policy periodically for updates.


Information we collect

We collect information from users automatically when they use our app.

Our third parties, such as Microsoft, may collect additional information from and about users at their convenience. Review Microsoft's Privacy Statement here


Profile information

When registering for our app, users are required to create a profile. Information provided to set up a profile, such as a name or profile picture, is stored privately in the user's device. No one, not even LaughNow, has access to this information.


Streak and laughter attempts

LaughNow keeps track of how many times a user has laughed and how many times a user has tried to laugh. Both values are securely stored on the user's device and no one, including LaughNow, can see those values.


Additional information

We may request additional information to follow up on help tickets.

You may provide additional information when you contact us (such as your name, email address or phone number).

Additionally collected information will be stored until there's no further need for LaughNow to keep it (e.g. after closing a help ticket).


How we use your information

We use information that we collect about you or that you provide to us to:


  • Provide you with our app or other services that you request from us.

  • Meet any other purpose for which you provide it.

Changes to our privacy policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. If we complete modifications to it, we will publish the new privacy policy on this page.


Contact information

For more information on our privacy policy and privacy practices, contact us at: humans@laughnow.app
